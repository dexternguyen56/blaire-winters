Blaire Winters

Hi, I'm Blaire Winters! I legally gave myself this name and I live by the motto "doesn't make sense not to live for fun" from the song All Stars. My story didn't start out very fun, but through it all, 'fun' still remains my top priority.

Since childhood, everyone said that I was an artist at heart. Unfortunately, this was what my family had fear most, for they wanted a doctor at heart. We all know how that story goes.

Though my environment did not promote creative growth, I tried to keep the creative soul in me alive with any and every opportunity. At the age of 21, I finally risked everything to live my dream of becoming an ice skater, which unexpectedly help me grow as a creative storyteller through video-making.

Now I am a freestyle-level ice skater, videographer, photographer, editor, content creator, and social media manager.

Let us always live for fun!
